üè† 50% - Needs (Essential Expenses)

  • Housing: Rent/mortgage, property taxes, homeowners insurance (+8% since 2023)

  • Basic Groceries: Essential food items (not dining out) (+15% since 2023)
  • Healthcare: Insurance premiums, essential medications (+6% since 2023)
  • Minimum Debt Payments: Required payments on loans

üéØ 30% - Wants (Lifestyle Choices)

  • Subscriptions: Multiple streaming services, subscription boxes

  • Investments: Brokerage accounts, education funds
  • Large Purchases: Saving for car, home down payment

2025 Common Budgeting Mistakes and Solutions

  • Mistake: Not adjusting for 2025 inflation
    Solution: Increase budget categories by 3-5% for inflation
  • Mistake: Forgetting subscription creep
    Solution: Audit subscriptions quarterly using apps like Rocket Money
  • Mistake: Not having sinking funds for irregular expenses
    Solution: Create funds for car maintenance, holidays, medical
  • Mistake: Budgeting ideal numbers, not reality
    Solution: Track spending for 30 days before creating budget
  • Mistake: Giving up after one bad month
    Solution: Budgeting is a skill that improves with practice
