First Investments for 2025: Building Your Foundation

1. Total Stock Market ETF (VTI or ITOT)

These ETFs track the entire US stock market, giving you exposure to 3,500+ companies. Perfect foundation for any 2025 portfolio.

  • VTI Expense Ratio: 0.03% ($3 annually per $10,000 invested)
  • Companies Held: 3,500+ US stocks
  • 2025 Performance: +8.2% YTD projection
  • Minimum Investment: 1 share (~$250) or fractional shares

2. S&P 500 ETF (SPY or VOO)

Track the 500 largest US companies. A classic core holding updated for 2025 market conditions.

  • VOO Expense Ratio: 0.03%
  • Companies Held: 500 largest US companies
  • 2025 Performance: +7.8% YTD projection
  • Historical Returns: 10.5% average since 1926

3. Target Date Retirement Fund

"All-in-one" funds that automatically adjust your allocation. Perfect for 2025 beginners who want simplicity.

  • Example: Vanguard Target Retirement 2065 Fund (VLXVX)
  • Expense Ratio: 0.08%
  • Minimum Investment: $1,000
  • 2025 Allocation: 90% stocks, 10% bonds (for young investors)

2025 Advanced Beginner Strategies

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) in 2025

Investing fixed amounts regularly regardless of market conditions. This automatically buys more shares when prices are low and fewer when prices are high.

2025 Asset Allocation by Age

A simple rule: subtract your age from 110 to determine your stock allocation. Example: Age 25 = 85% stocks, 15% bonds. Adjust every 5 years.

Tax-Loss Harvesting for 2025

Selling investments at a loss to offset capital gains taxes. Advanced strategy for taxable accounts once you have significant gains.
